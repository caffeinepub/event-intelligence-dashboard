Event Type Mix by Year

How DFINITY's event format strategy has evolved
Hackathon volume spiked in 2023 (86 events – ICP Hub-driven across India, Africa, LATAM) before sharply contracting to just 7 in 2024. VIP and exclusive events grew through 2024 as focus shifted toward investor and institutional audiences. Hackathons are returning in 2025 but in a targeted, DFINITY-direct format.
